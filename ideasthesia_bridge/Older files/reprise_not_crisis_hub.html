Corporate Mission, Briefly

Reprise, Not Crisis Ltd. exists for the orderly handling of theoretically interesting situations, editorially overgrown projects, canon/legacy disentanglement, and moments in which a body of work has begun speaking in multiple dialects at once.

The company recognises that not every alarming circumstance constitutes a crisis. Reprise remains available in most cases.
