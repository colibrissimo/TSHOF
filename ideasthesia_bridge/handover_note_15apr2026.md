## One thing worth knowing

The JOY signal is real but weaker than initially reported. The
stronger claim is about invariance, not modulation. The emotions
that don't move (SADNESS, PEACEFUL, GRIEF, TERRIFIED, DESPERATION)
are the more defensible and more interesting result: their geometric
properties are intrinsic to the concepts, robust to both relational
manipulation and architectural variation.

The preliminary analysis error was an honest one (likely a
transcription error during hand-compilation under time pressure),
but it matters because the "unanimous 5/5" framing made the finding
sound categorical. At 4/5 vs 2/5, it is a trend, not a categorical
shift. The write-up now reflects this.
